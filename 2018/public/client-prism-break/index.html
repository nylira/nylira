PRISM Break

PRISM Break is an open source project aimed at reducing the impact of government internet surveillance by offering recommendations to popular closed source software and services. Nylira built the initial site from scratch and people around the world have contributed to localizations and project recommendations. Every page on the site is entirely responsive, and it's 100% functional without JavaScript. Visit site.
